Tray 5 (Bypass) Feed Edge

Feed Edge or Bypass Tray Feed Edge determines how paper feeds into the printer.

To specify Tray 5 (Bypass) Feed Edge:

In the Advanced window, next to Tray 5 (Bypass) Feed Edge, click the arrows, then select an option.

  • Automatically Select allows the printer to print using any tray loaded with the size and type of paper required for the print job.
  • Long Edge Feed (Normal) feeds paper into the printer on the long edge.
  • Short Edge Feed feeds paper into the printer on the short edge. Use this setting to print labels, staple another part of the page, or print on hole punched paper.
