Output Destination

To select an output destination:

  1. In the Paper/Output window, next to Output Destination, click the arrows, then select an option.
  2. To send the print job to a destination based on the paper size and finishing options, select Auto or Automatically Select.

    Notes:

    • Based on other settings, some paper trays can be unavailable.
    • If your printer has only one output destination, this list does not appear.
