Trimming Adjustments

To adjust the trim:

  1. In the Trimming Adjustment window, make trimming adjustments as needed.
    • Vertical Position: Trims the book block vertically.
    • Top Rotation: Allows for fine adjustment of the top cutter head.
    • Front Rotation: Allows for fine adjustment of the fore edge cutter head. This adjustment ensures that the fore edge of the book is parallel to the bind edge.
    • Bottom Rotation: Allows for fine adjustment of the bottom cutter head.
  2. Click OK.
