Send a Fax to a Phonebook Recipient

You can add a fax recipient from the phonebook to the fax.

To add a recipient from your phonebook to the fax:

  1. In the Paper/Output window, next to Job Type, click the arrows, then select Fax.
  2. Click Recipients.
  3. Click the address book icon, then do one of the following:
    • Select a group, then select a recipient.
    • To locate a recipient, type the name of the recipient in the search box, then click OK.
  4. Click OK.
