Add or Edit a Fax Recipient

After you create fax recipient records, you can add the records to your personal phonebook.

To add recipient records:

  1. In the Paper/Output window, next to Job Type, click the arrows, then select Fax.
  2. Click Recipients.
  3. Click the Add Recipient button.
  4. Type the contact information.
  5. Click OK.

To edit a fax recipient without editing the phonebook record:

  1. In the Paper/Output window, next to Job Type, click the arrows, then select Fax.
  2. Click Recipients.
  3. Select the recipient in the table and click Edit.
  4. Change the information as needed.
  5. Click OK.
