Thin Line Enhancement

Thin Line Enhancement adds thickness to thin lines to ensure that even the thinnest lines print.

Note: Not all options listed are supported on all printers. Some options apply only to a specific printer model, configuration, operating system, or driver type.

To use Thin Line Enhancement:

In the Advanced window, next to Thin Line Enhancement, click the arrows, then select an option.

  • On: Adds thickness to thin lines.
  • Off: Turns off Thin Line Enhancement.
