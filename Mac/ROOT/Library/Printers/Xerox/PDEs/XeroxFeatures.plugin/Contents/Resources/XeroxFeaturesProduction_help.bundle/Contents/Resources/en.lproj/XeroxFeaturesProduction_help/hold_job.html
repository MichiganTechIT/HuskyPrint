Hold Job

Hold Job keeps the job at the printer until you release it at the control panel. The print job is not saved on the print server after it is released for printing.

To hold a print job:

  1. In the Paper/Output window, next to Job Type, click the arrows, then select Hold Print.
  2. Make other selections for the print job as needed.
  3. Click Print. The job remains in the job list on the print server until it is released for printing.
