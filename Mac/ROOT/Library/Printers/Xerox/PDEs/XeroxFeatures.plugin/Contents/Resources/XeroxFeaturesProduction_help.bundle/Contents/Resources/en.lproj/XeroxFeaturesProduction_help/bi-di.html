Bi-Directional Communication

Use the Bi-Directional Communication Setup to enable two-way communication between a network printer and your computer.

Note: Not all options listed are supported on all printers. Some options apply only to a specific printer model, configuration, operating system, or driver type.

To enable or disable bi-directional communication:

  1. In the Advanced window, next to Configuration, click Bi-Directional Communication.
  2. Next to Bi-Directional Communication, select On or Off.
  3. Click OK.
