Fax Cover Sheet

To include a cover sheet with your fax:

  1. In the Paper/Output window, next to Job Type, click the arrows, then select Fax.
  2. Click Cover Sheet.
  3. Next to Cover Sheet, click the arrows, then select Print a Cover Sheet.
  4. To add a message to recipients, next to Cover Sheet Notes, click the button.
  5. To add your contact information, next to Sender Information, click the button.
  6. To edit the recipient information, next to Recipient Information, click the button.
  7. To specify a Cover sheet Image, click the arrows, then select an option.
    • To turn off Cover Sheet Images, select None.
    • To select a Cover Sheet Image option saved previously to the printer, select it from the list.
    • To edit a previously saved Cover Sheet Image, select it from the list, then click Edit.
    • To create a new Cover Sheet Image, click New.
  8. Click Cover Sheet Paper Size, then select an option.
  9. Click OK.
