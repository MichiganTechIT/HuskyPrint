Square Fold Finishing

The Square Fold feature allows you to customize the fold of the spine of square folded booklets.

To select Square Fold options:

  1. In the Square Fold window, next to Square Fold, select an option.
    • To specify a more square fold, select from Normal to +2.
    • To specify a rounder fold, select from Normal to -2.
    • To turn off Square Fold, select Off.
  2. Click OK.
