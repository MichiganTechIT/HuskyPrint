Trim Outside Edge

The Trim Outside Edge feature specifies how much to trim from the outside edge of a booklet.

To trim the outside edge:

  1. To enable Trim Outside Edge, select the check box.
  2. Next to Trim Outside Edge, type a value or use the arrows to select a value to trim the edge.
  3. To specify the units of measurement, in the units field, click the arrows, then select in. or mm.
  4. Click OK.
