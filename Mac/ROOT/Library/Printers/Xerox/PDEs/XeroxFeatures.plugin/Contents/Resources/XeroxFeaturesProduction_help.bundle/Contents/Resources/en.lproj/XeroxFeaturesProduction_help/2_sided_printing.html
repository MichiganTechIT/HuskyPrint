2-Sided Printing

In the Paper/Output window, next to 2-sided printing, click the arrows, then select an option.

  • 1-Sided Print: Prints on one side of the media. Use this option when printing on transparencies, envelopes, labels, or other media that cannot be printed on both sides.
  • 2-Sided Print: Prints on both sides of the paper to allow binding on the long edge of the paper.
  • 2-Sided Print, Flip on Short Edge: Prints on both sides of the paper to allow binding on the short edge of the paper.
