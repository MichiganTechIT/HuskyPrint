Recipient Information

To add or edit recipient information:

In the Recipient Information window, select an option.

  • Show Recipients: Displays the recipient names on the fax cover sheet. The information also appears on the Cover Sheet preview.
  • Do Not Show Recipients: Conceals the recipient names on the fax cover sheet.
  • Show Custom Text: Allows you to type custom text in the text box that prints on the fax cover sheet.
